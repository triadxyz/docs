# Pre-Market

**The gamified onboarding layer that lets anyone predict for free.**

---

## What is Pre-Market?

Pre-Market is Triad's **zero-risk entry point** into prediction markets. It allows users to participate in markets **before they officially launch** — without depositing any funds.

Think of it as a training ground where your predictions are real, your positions are tracked, and your projected earnings are calculated — all before you put a single dollar at risk.

---

## How It Works

### 1. Discover Markets via the Swipe Feed

Pre-Market uses a **Vertical Swipe Feed** — similar to Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts — to present markets to users. Each card shows a prediction question, and users simply **swipe to predict** YES or NO.

This UX is designed to feel native to Gen Z and Gen Alpha audiences who are already fluent in swipe-based interactions.

### 2. Enter Positions for Free

When you make a prediction in Pre-Market, you enter a position **without any upfront cost**. There is no balance required. Your position is recorded and tracked in real time.

### 3. See Your Projected Earnings

As the market evolves, Pre-Market calculates your **Projected Earnings** — the amount you would earn if your prediction is correct. This creates excitement and engagement before any money is involved.

### 4. Convert to a Real Position

When a Pre-Market officially launches as a live market, your position is **automatically converted** to an active one. At this point, you are invited to deposit funds based on your projected earnings.

## Why Pre-Market Matters

| Traditional Onboarding | Pre-Market Onboarding |
| :--- | :--- |
| Requires deposit first | Zero cost to start |
| High friction (KYC, wallet setup) | Swipe-based, instant entry |
| Users must understand prediction markets | Users learn by doing |
| Drop-off at deposit stage | Conversion based on projected profit |

---

## Key Benefits

- **Zero Risk** — Users enter positions without committing any balance.
- **Intuitive UX** — The swipe feed makes predictions feel like social media.
- **Natural Conversion** — Users convert when they see real projected earnings, not when asked to deposit blindly.
- **Education Through Action** — Instead of reading docs, users learn to predict by actually predicting.

---

## Who is Pre-Market For?

Pre-Market is designed for **everyone** — especially users who:

- Have never used a prediction market before
- Are curious but not ready to deposit
- Want to test their intuition before committing capital
- Come from Web2 platforms and expect a mobile-first, frictionless experience
