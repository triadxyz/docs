### Collection Rarities

Each Poseidon has an assigned rarity that influences its value and the utilities it offers. The rarities are distributed as follows:

- Mythic: 1% of NFTs
- Legendary: 5% of NFTs
- Epic: 15% of NFTs
- Rare: 35% of NFTs
- Uncommon: 60% of NFTs
- Common: 100% of NFTs

Within the collection, some NFTs feature exclusive Badges, making them even more valuable. Only those who minted and kept their NFT staked received this special Badge. Since this benefit can no longer be earned, these NFTs are extremely rare and highly sought after in the secondary market.
