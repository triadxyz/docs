# Referral

_In Atlantis, every traveler is a vital part of our ecosystem. With our referral system, you can expand your influence and be rewarded for it. By sharing your unique link, each new adventurer who joins us through it brings a special blessing: you earn 10% in Boost from their activities._


Think of this as a chain of friendship that strengthens with each new participant. As more travelers join our journey, your rewards grow, reflecting the collaborative spirit of Atlantis. The more friends you bring, the more Boost you accumulate, enhancing your achievements and experiences. Join this tradition of camaraderie and strengthen your presence in the depths of the great underwater city!
