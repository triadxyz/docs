# Prediction Markets

Prediction markets allow participants to trade shares representing the likelihood of future events. These markets harness **collective intelligence**, enabling speculation on politics, sports, business, technology, cryptocurrencies, and cultural events.

---

## How It Works

Triad empowers you to act on your predictions by choosing between two options:

- **YES** — Predict that the event **will happen**. If you are right, you earn rewards based on the accuracy of your forecast.
- **NO** — Predict that the event **won't happen**. If your prediction holds true, you receive rewards proportional to the outcome.

The price of a share reflects the aggregated probability the market assigns to a particular outcome. For example, if a share is priced at **$0.70**, it suggests a **70% chance** of that outcome occurring.

---

## 1:1 Payout Mechanism

All Triad markets are resolved on a **1:1 basis**: when a market closes, winners receive an amount equivalent to what participants on the opposite side contributed, based on the probability of the event.

### How It Works

1. **Predictions** — Users predict specific outcomes. Participants on different sides contribute to a shared pool.
2. **Market Probability** — The more participants predict one outcome, the lower the reward (higher probability). Lower-probability predictions offer higher potential rewards.
3. **Resolution** — After the event concludes, winners receive their share. Losers forfeit their contributions.

### Example

- Two options: **"Outcome A"** and **"Outcome B"**
- **1,000 USDC** is contributed to "Outcome A" and **2,000 USDC** to "Outcome B"
- If "Outcome A" is correct, participants who predicted "Outcome B" lose their contributions, which are paid to "Outcome A" holders proportionally.

## Fees

### Trading Fees (Buy & Sell)

Triad uses a dynamic fee system based on share price:

| Price Range | Fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| **$0.10 – $0.90** | 3.8% (standard) |
| **$0.91 – $0.99** | 1.9% → 0.9% (reduced) |

The higher the price, the lower the fee — encouraging trading in balanced markets.

### Payout Fee

- **If you win:** A **0.36% fee** is charged **only on profits** (not on your original investment).
- **If you lose:** **No fee** is charged.

### Example — $1,000 Investment

1. **Entry:** You pay the trading fee (0.9% to 3.8% depending on price).
2. **Win:** Invested $1,000, won $2,000 → Fee of **$7.20** (0.36% on $2,000 profit).
3. **Loss:** No additional fee.
