# Affiliate Program

**Earn USDC by referring new users to Triadmarkets.**

---

## How It Works

1. **Connect your wallet** on [triadfi.co](https://triadfi.co).
2. A **unique referral code** is automatically generated for your account.
3. **Share your link** with friends, followers, or your community.
4. **Earn 5%** of all trading fees paid by your direct referrals.

---

## Earnings

| Detail              | Value                         |
| :------------------ | :---------------------------- |
| **Commission rate** | 5% of referral's trading fees |
| **Currency**        | USDC                          |
| **Minimum payout**  | $5.00                         |
| **Payout method**   | Automatic to your wallet      |
| **Network fees**    | 100% covered by Triad         |

### How Payouts Work

- Commissions accumulate in real time as your referrals trade.
- Once your balance exceeds **$5.00**, payouts are sent **automatically** to your connected wallet.
- **Triad covers 100% of network fees** for affiliate payouts — you receive the full amount.

> **Coming soon:** The minimum payout threshold is planned to decrease to **$1.00** in a future update.

---

## Tips for Maximizing Earnings

- **Share on social media** — Post your referral link on Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and other channels.
- **Create content** — Tutorials, reviews, and guides about Triad can drive referral activity.
- **Engage your community** — The more active your referrals, the more fees they generate and the more you earn.

---

## Transparency

All referral tracking and payouts are processed **on-chain**, ensuring full transparency. You can verify your earnings at any time through your Triad dashboard.
