# First Access

**Two ways to join Triadmarkets — choose whichever suits you best.**

---

## Option 1: Email Access (Recommended for New Users)

The fastest way to get started. No crypto wallet required.

### How It Works

1. **Go to** [triadfi.co](https://triadfi.co) and click **Sign Up**.
2. **Enter your email** or sign in with **Google**.
3. **Create a security PIN** — This protects your account and transactions.
4. A **smart wallet** is automatically created for you using **Phantom** technology.

That's it. You now have a fully functional Solana wallet linked to your email, with no extensions to install and no seed phrases to manage.

> **Note:** Your smart wallet is custody-free — only you have access. The PIN ensures that no one else can approve transactions on your behalf.

---

## Option 2: Connect a Solana Wallet

For experienced crypto users who already have a Solana wallet.

### Supported Wallets

- **Phantom**
- **Solflare**
- **Backpack**
- And other Solana-compatible wallets

### How to Connect

1. **Go to** [triadfi.co](https://triadfi.co).
2. Click **Connect Wallet**.
3. **Select your wallet** from the list and approve the connection.
4. You are now connected and ready to trade.

---

## Self-Custody & Transparency

Regardless of which method you choose, Triadmarkets operates on a **self-custody model**:

- **Your keys, your funds** — Triadmarkets never holds your assets.
- **On-chain transparency** — Every transaction is recorded on the Solana blockchain and can be verified at any time.
- **No intermediaries** — Deposits, predictions, and withdrawals are handled directly through smart contracts.
