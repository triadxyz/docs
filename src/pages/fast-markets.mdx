# Fast Markets

**High-frequency prediction markets with 15-minute cycles.**

---

## What are Fast Markets?

Fast Markets are live prediction markets designed for **short-term, real-time outcomes**. Users speculate on rapid events such as instant fluctuations in cryptocurrency prices.

**Example:**

> "Will Bitcoin be above $115,312 at 9:15 PM?"
>
> - If the price is **above** that value at the specified time and you voted **YES**, you win.
> - If the price is **not above**, the users who chose **NO** win.

---

## How It Works

1. **Choose an asset** — Select from **BTC**, **ETH**, or **SOL**.
2. **Read the question** — Each round presents a clear price prediction question.
3. **Place your prediction** — Vote **YES** or **NO** based on your analysis.
4. **Wait for resolution** — Rounds last **15 minutes** and restart automatically.
5. **Collect winnings** — If your prediction is correct, rewards are distributed automatically.

---

## Price Data

Fast Markets use **real-time price data** from the **Pyth Network**, ensuring fairness, accuracy, and transparency in every result.

> **Note:** Prices may vary between aggregators due to differences in liquidity, oracle structures, and consulted pools. To ensure fairness, **only the Pyth price at the exact moment of resolution** determines the outcome.

---

## Rules and Details

### Resolution

- Market resolution is based **exclusively** on price data provided by the **Pyth Network** via Triad.
- Resolution occurs automatically at the end of each 15-minute round.

### Payouts

- Each market is resolved within **24 hours**, and payouts are processed within **24 hours after resolution**.
- Resolution occurs the following day, according to the rules defined for each market.

### Real-Time Charts

Each Fast Market includes a **real-time chart** that allows users to analyze price trends before placing their prediction. This helps users make informed decisions based on current momentum.

---

## Transparency

All results are determined by **verifiable, on-chain data**. The entire process is executed through **smart contracts** — no intermediaries, no manipulation.
