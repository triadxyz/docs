# NFT FAQ

## Where can I buy Poseidons?

You can acquire our NFTs at: https://www.tensor.trade/trade/poseidons

## Are the rarities for staking collected from Tensor or Magic Eden?

They are collected from Tensor. We don't use Magic Eden for any NFT features.

## The rarer my NFT is, the more it receives rewards?

The rarer the NFT, the more it earns. The multiplier is base in the Rarity Rank with the badge you have more 3x in the rewards.

## What is this badge that some NFTs have?

This badge is a bonus that provides 3x the value that the NFT in rewards, plus zero fees on operations in our protocol.

## What are NFT rewards?

NFT rewards are a portion of the fees collected in each marketplace. The total fee applied to NFTs is 0.1%.

## What are the benefits of my NFT?

Great rewards in $TRD and access to airdrops
