# Founders

The team behind Triad Markets.

## Davi Cardoso — CEO

Co-founder and CEO. Davi leads business strategy, partnerships, and overall company direction. His vision for making prediction markets accessible to mainstream audiences drives Triad's product roadmap and growth strategy.

## Daniel Cardoso — CTO

Co-founder and CTO. Daniel architects Triad's technical infrastructure, from the on-chain Solana programs to the real-time market resolution systems. He ensures the platform maintains institutional-grade reliability while remaining fast and cost-efficient.

## Neto Monte — Head of Product

Co-founder and Head of Product. Neto designs the user experience that makes Triad feel invisible to Web3 newcomers. From the swipe-based Pre-Market interface to the gamification systems, he focuses on making complex financial instruments feel as simple as using a social app.
