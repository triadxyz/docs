# Introduction to the Season Points

### What are Season Points? 

Season Points are points accumulated by TriadFi users over a "season" . During this time, participants must complete the available quests on the website, earning points for:

- Saying "Gm" in our Discord 
- Staking a certain amount of $TRD 
- Swapping to $TRD 
- Staking the NFT for a set period 
- And various other interactive activities with the platform.
