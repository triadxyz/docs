# Levels & Score

**Earn XP, climb levels, and maximize your rewards through consistent prediction activity.**

---

## Level System

Every action on Triadmarkets earns **XP (Experience Points)** that contribute to your level progression. Higher levels unlock better rewards and recognition within the platform.

### XP Sources

| Action             | XP Type                   | Description                                   |
| :----------------- | :------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------- |
| Place a prediction | **Prediction XP**         | Earned every time you enter a market position |
| Win a prediction   | **Correct Prediction XP** | Bonus XP for accurate predictions             |
| Provide liquidity  | **Liquidity Provider XP** | Earned by supplying liquidity to markets      |

---

## Dynamic Score

Your **Score** represents your overall value as a participant on Triadmarkets. It combines your level, activity, and prediction accuracy into a single metric.

### Activity Multiplier

To reward consistent engagement and prevent inactive accounts from farming rewards, Triadmarkets uses an **Activity Multiplier** based on a **rolling 30-day window**.

| Activity Level                    | Multiplier      |
| :-------------------------------- | :-------------- |
| Highly active (daily predictions) | **8.0x**        |
| Regularly active                  | **4.0x – 6.0x** |
| Occasionally active               | **2.0x – 3.0x** |
| Minimum activity                  | **1.0x**        |

### How Decay Works

- Your multiplier remains at maximum as long as you stay active.
- After **14 days of inactivity**, the multiplier begins to decrease.
- After **30 days of inactivity**, the multiplier hits its minimum (1.0x).
- **Any new activity immediately resets the decay clock.**

---

## Effective Score & Rewards

Your **Effective Score** determines your share of monthly rewards:

> **Effective Score = Score × Activity Multiplier**

### Monthly Rewards

Rewards are distributed monthly based on each user's **Effective Score** relative to the total pool. The higher your score and the more consistent your activity, the larger your share of the reward pool.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Predict regularly** to maintain a high Activity Multiplier.
- **Win predictions** to earn bonus XP and climb levels faster.
- **Stay active** — even one prediction prevents multiplier decay.
- **Your Effective Score** directly determines your monthly rewards.
