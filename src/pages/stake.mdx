## Key Features of Triad Staking

### Daily Rewards

Rewards are distributed **per second** and calculated based on:

* **Time staked**
* **Amount of $TRD staked**

### $TRD Token Staking

* Stake **$TRD tokens** directly.
* Earn **interest** on your staked balance.

### Lock-up Periods

* **$TRD tokens**: **7-day lock-up** period.
