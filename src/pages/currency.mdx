# Deposits & Withdrawals

**All activity on Triadmarkets is conducted in USDC on the Solana network.**

---

## Deposits

### Step 1: Acquire USDC

Purchase **USDC** on a centralized exchange such as:

- **Binance**
- **OKX**
- **Bybit**
- **Coinbase**
- Or any exchange that supports **USDC on Solana**

### Step 2: Transfer to Your Solana Wallet

Send USDC from the exchange to your Solana wallet (e.g., **Phantom**, **Solflare**, **Backpack**).

> **Important:** Make sure to select the **Solana network** when withdrawing from the exchange. Sending USDC on the wrong network (e.g., Ethereum) will result in lost funds.

### Step 3: Connect to Triadmarkets

Once your wallet has USDC:

1. Go to [triadfi.co](https://triadfi.co).
2. Connect your wallet.
3. Your USDC balance will be available automatically.

### Need to Swap Tokens?

If you have SOL or other tokens and need USDC, use **[Jupiter](https://jup.ag)** — the best swap aggregator on Solana — to convert your tokens to USDC instantly.

---

## Withdrawals

### How to Withdraw

1. **Access your balance** on Triadmarkets.
2. Click **Withdraw**.
3. **Enter the destination wallet address** (must be a Solana wallet).
4. **Enter the amount** you wish to withdraw.
5. **Confirm the transaction.**

Your USDC will be sent directly to your specified wallet.

---

## Important Notes

### SOL for Network Fees

You need a small amount of **SOL** in your wallet to cover Solana network transaction fees (typically less than $0.01 per transaction). Without SOL, you won't be able to approve transactions.

### Security Best Practices

- **Double-check wallet addresses** before sending or withdrawing funds.
- **Never share your private key or seed phrase** with anyone.
- **Use a hardware wallet** for large balances when possible.
- Only interact with **official Triadmarkets links** to avoid phishing.
