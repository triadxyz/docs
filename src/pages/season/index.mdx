# Atlantis Season - Triad: Points & Rewards

The Atlantis Season of Triad is the most ambitious yet, bringing an exciting mix of rewards, quests, and multipliers that encourage active participation on the platform. Tridents, Triad's prestige points, will be distributed as users complete daily, weekly, and seasonal missions, as well as through trading, staking, and other essential activities within the Triad ecosystem.

### How to Earn Tridents

Users can accumulate Tridents in multiple ways:

- **Trading in $TRD pairs**
- **Swaps on the Jupiter platform**
- **Participating in challenges and special events**
- **Staking TRD and eligible NFTs**
