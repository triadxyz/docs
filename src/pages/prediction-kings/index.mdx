## Introduction

**Prediction Kings – Triad’s Weekly Volume Trading Competition**

Prediction Kings is Triad’s weekly competition, rewarding users with the highest trading volume on the platform. Each campaign runs for 7 days, with a real-time leaderboard tracking participants’ performance.

- The leaderboard highlights the top 10 participants, ranked by their total weekly trading volume.

- The top 3 traders receive exclusive cards showcasing their total volume, PNL, and estimated rewards.

- The more you trade, the better your chances of climbing the ranks and earning $TRD rewards.
