# Introduction to Triadmarkets

Triadmarkets is a **decentralized prediction market platform** built on the Solana blockchain, where users speculate on real-world outcomes across **politics, sports, crypto, entertainment, technology**, and more.

---

## Why Triadmarkets?

### Institutional-Grade Infrastructure

All markets settle on-chain with full transparency and near-zero latency, powered by Solana's high-throughput architecture. Every transaction is verifiable on the blockchain.

### Pre-Markets: Zero-Risk Onboarding

New users can participate in prediction markets **for free** before committing capital. Pre-Market positions convert automatically upon official launch, eliminating the traditional deposit barrier.

### Sustainable Economics

Triadmarkets operates on a **Real Yield** model. Revenue generated by the protocol drives token buybacks and staker distributions — no inflationary emissions.

### Gamified Progression

Every prediction earns **XP** that feeds into a **Level** and **Score** system. Consistent, skilled predictors are rewarded with multipliers and monthly payouts.

### Invisible Web3

Triadmarkets removes blockchain complexity so anyone can participate. Email sign-up, smart wallets, and a mobile-first UX make predictions as simple as swiping through a social feed.

---

## Core Products

- **Prediction Markets** — Standard markets resolved on a 1:1 payout basis with transparent fee structures.
- **Fast Markets** — High-frequency, 15-minute prediction rounds on BTC, ETH, and SOL using real-time Pyth Network data.
- **Pre-Markets** — Free-to-play gamified onboarding that converts attention into real positions.
