# Introduction to Triad

Triad is a decentralized prediction market platform built on the Solana blockchain, allowing users to speculate on real-world events and profit from their insights. On Triad, you can trade predictions on __politics, pop culture, business, sports, technology, cryptocurrencies, DeFi, and NFTs__, turning your market insights into impactful strategies.

With markets designed to reflect global and regional events, The platform is designed to provide transparency, speed, and accessibility, enabling anyone to participate and take advantage of prediction market opportunities., leveraging Solana's advanced infrastructure. 

## First Access

Our platform offers two login methods for greater convenience and security: using the Phantom wallet with an email to create a new wallet or connecting an existing Solana wallet. Below, we detail each option.

### 1. Access via Phantom Wallet with Email


**If you don’t have a Solana wallet yet, you can create a new one using the Phantom service directly through our platform. Follow the steps below:**

1. Click the **"Sign Up"** button on the homepage.
2. Select **"Sign up or Login"**, then **"Continue with Google"**.
3. Create a **security PIN** for your wallet.
4. Confirm and click **"Get Started"**.
5. Done! Your wallet is ready to **use the platform**.


### 2. Access via Existing Solana Wallet

**If you already have a Solana wallet installed as a browser extension or on your mobile device, follow these steps to connect:**

1. On the homepage, click **"Sign Up or floating phantom button"**.
2. Choose your preferred wallet (e.g., **Phantom**, **Solflare**, or others).
3. Approve the connection request from your wallet.
4. You're now **connected** and ready to **use the platform**.

With these two login methods, we ensure that all users—whether beginners or experienced in the Solana ecosystem—can access our platform securely and efficiently. If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team!
