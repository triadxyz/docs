# Progression System

## How Points are Claimed

Missions points are managed **automatically**:

* Tasks are counted **automatically** upon completion.
* Wager points are added every **24 hours**.

### How to unlock Triad Points

To unlock and claim your daily points, you must place at least **one prediction** on that day.

This means that each day, making a new prediction is required to **activate and claim** your total daily point accumulation.
### In Short:

> **1 daily prediction** = **daily points unlocked.**

## Point Booster

Seasonal multipliers are boosted by your **NFTs**, allowing you to maximize your gains:

* **Poseidon NFTs:** Multiplier based on total combined power, up to **6x**.
* **Collection NFT:** Grants **1.05x**.
* **Triad Pickaxe:** Grants **1.5x**.

## Achievements

### How it Works

* Unlock unique achievements by reaching **specific milestones** on the platform.
* Each achievement rewards you with **bonus points**.
* Achievements can only be earned **once per account**.

### Achievement Examples

Milestones that unlock achievements include:

* Reaching trading volume milestones
* Building winning streaks
* Providing liquidity
* Owning Poseidon NFTs
* Completing seasonal challenges
* Inviting friends
* Trading in Fast Markets
* Participating in airdrops

**Action:** Track your progress and **unlock all achievements for maximum rewards**.
