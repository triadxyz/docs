# The Triad Markets Story

From On-Chain Experiment to the World's Most Accessible Prediction Infrastructure.

## Overview

Founded in early 2024 by **Davi Cardoso**, **Daniel Cardoso**, and **Neto Monte**, Triad was born with a mission to transform market intelligence into tradable assets. What started as a technical tool for pricing protocols on the Solana network has evolved into a prediction infrastructure connecting Web3 liquidity to mainstream audiences.

## 2024: Origins and the On-Chain Alpha Experiment

Triad's journey began with a technical challenge: **how to measure the real value of a protocol's "hype" or failure using real-time on-chain data?**

The initial platform allowed users to trade based on project growth — an innovation that laid the foundation for what Triad is today. Operating on Solana, the team proved that high-frequency, low-cost, instant-execution prediction markets were possible.

However, the founders knew the project's true potential lay beyond the crypto bubble.

## 2025: The Web2 Revolution and the "El Gato Effect"

2025 marked the biggest strategic pivot in the company's history: **the Web2 Discovery**.

Triad realized that prediction markets were a universal language of entertainment, but the technology needed to be **invisible to everyday users**. The project broke through blockchain's technical barriers and focused on partnerships with digital entertainment giants.

The entry of heavyweight partners like **El Gato** — one of Brazil's biggest names in gaming and streaming — validated this thesis. Through campaigns that spoke the language of mass audiences, Triad brought thousands of new users into the ecosystem, proving that prediction markets could be as simple and engaging as social media.

### Key Milestones

- **Pre-Markets Launch**: Free-to-play mode that lets users predict before depositing.
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaboration with major content creators to drive mainstream adoption.
- **Mobile-First UX**: Swipe-based interface designed for Gen Z and Gen Alpha.

## 2026: Institutional Scale and Product Evolution

In 2026, Triad evolves from a consumer product into an **institutional-grade prediction infrastructure**:

- **Whitelabel Platform**: Any brand, media company, or influencer can launch their own prediction market powered by Triad's engine.
- **$TRIAD Token**: Real Yield tokenomics with revenue-driven buybacks and burns.
- **Fast Markets**: High-frequency 15-minute prediction rounds on BTC, ETH, and SOL.
- **Cross-Chain Expansion**: Exploring multi-chain settlement beyond Solana.

## The Present: The Future of Prediction Markets

Triad is building the **Rails of Prediction** — an infrastructure layer where any topic, event, or real-world outcome can become a tradable market.

The vision is simple: **make predictions as natural as posting on social media**, while maintaining the transparency, speed, and fairness that only blockchain can provide.
